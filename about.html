Our Story

Brewed Awakening was founded in 2018 by two college friends, Emma and Liam, who shared a passion for exceptional coffee. What started as a tiny corner shop has grown into one of the most beloved cafés in the neighbourhood.

Every decision we make is guided by one belief: a great café should feel like a second home. We work with small-scale farmers to ensure our coffee is ethically sourced and sustainably grown.

Our Journey

  1. 2018 — Founded by Emma and Liam
  2. 2019 — Launched our homemade pastry menu
  3. 2020 — Introduced single-origin coffee beans
  4. 2022 — Opened our dog-friendly outdoor terrace
  5. 2024 — Won Best Local Café at the Community Choice Awards
